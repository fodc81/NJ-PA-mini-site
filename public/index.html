FAQ

Common Questions Answered

Outdoor movie rental in New Jersey starts at $399 for indoor screenings and $449 for the Backyard Movie Package. Mid-tier packages like the Premiere Movie Package run $799 and the Blockbuster Package is $899. Large-scale drive-in and community festival setups with screens up to 52 feet start at $2,300. All packages include delivery, professional setup, full tech operation during the event, and teardown — no hidden fees.
We offer professional inflatable movie screens ranging from 16 feet to 52 feet wide. Smaller screens (16–20 ft) are ideal for backyard parties and residential events. Mid-size screens (20–30 ft) are perfect for school movie nights and corporate events. Giant screens (30–52 ft) are used for community festivals, drive-in experiences, and large outdoor events across New Jersey and Philadelphia.
Every Premiere Outdoor Movies package includes a complimentary weekday rain date at no extra charge. If weather forces a cancellation, we work with you to reschedule your event. New Jersey weather can be unpredictable — this guarantee is built into every booking to give you complete peace of mind.
For a standard backyard movie night, you need a flat open area of approximately 30 × 30 feet for our entry-level 16-foot screen. A 20-foot screen needs roughly 40 × 40 feet. Larger screens require open fields or parking lots. Our team will assess your space during the booking process and recommend the ideal screen size — no guesswork needed.
Public or semi-public outdoor screenings of copyrighted films require a public performance license, which is separate from the equipment rental. We advise all clients on licensing requirements and can direct you to the appropriate licensing services. Private residential screenings for family and friends typically do not require a commercial license. We'll walk you through the process during booking.
We recommend booking 4 to 6 weeks in advance for weekend dates, especially during summer (May–September). Holidays like Memorial Day, July 4th, and Labor Day fill up fast. Corporate events and large community festivals should be booked 2–3 months ahead. Last-minute bookings may be available — call us at 877-767-4338 to check availability.
We serve all of New Jersey and the greater Philadelphia area. In New Jersey: Newark, Jersey City, Hoboken, Trenton, Princeton, Edison, Toms River, Cherry Hill, Camden, Atlantic City, and throughout the Jersey Shore. In Pennsylvania: Philadelphia, Bucks County, Montgomery County, Chester County, Delaware County, and the Main Line suburbs. We also serve Wilmington, Delaware.
